- **ID:** `design-system.md`

- **Date:** October 5, 2025
- **Author:** Gemini, Project Architect

## 1.0 Design Philosophy & Mission
- **System Name:** "Apple-Inspired Trust & Clarity"
- **Mission:** To visually and textually guide users from a state of conflict to a state of resolution. Our tagline is "From Conflict to Clarity."
- **Core Principles:**
  - **De-escalation by Design:** The UI must feel calm, clean, and in control to reduce user anxiety.
  - **Trust & Transparency:** Components and layouts should be clear, honest, and provide easy access to security and privacy information.
  - **Minimalist Aesthetic:** Every element must have a purpose. Avoid clutter and use generous white space to guide the eye.

## 2.0 Global Design Rules

### 2.1 Color Palette
- **Primary Blue:** `#5D5FEF` (Branding, Positive Actions, Links)
- **Accent Green:** `#28A745` (Success States)
- **Standard Text:** `#333333`
- **Subtle Borders / Backgrounds:** `#E2E8F0` (Tailwind `gray-200`)
- **White:** `#FFFFFF`

### 2.2 Typography
- **Font Family:** Nunito
- **Icon Library:** Heroicons
- **Headlines (h1, h2, h3):** `font-bold`, `tracking-tight`
  - `h1`: `text-4xl` to `text-6xl`
  - `h2`: `text-3xl` to `text-4xl`
- **Body Text (p):** `text-lg`, `leading-8`

### 2.3 Brand & UX Rules
- **Stylized Brand Name:** In the brand name "Disagreement.AI," the word "agreement" **MUST** be styled in Primary Blue. The "Dis" prefix and ".AI" suffix **MUST** be the Standard Text color.
- **Linguistic Reinforcement:** The words "Agree" and "Agreement," when used as positive action words (like on buttons or links), **MUST** be styled in Primary Blue.

## 3.0 Global Component Styles

### 3.1 Buttons
- **Marketing Site (Tailwind CSS):** Pill-shaped (`rounded-full`). Primary CTAs must use the Primary Blue background with high-contrast white text.
- **In-App (Chakra UI):** `rounded="lg"`. Primary CTAs must use `colorScheme="blue"`.

### 3.2 Cards / Containers
- **Style:** `rounded="xl"` with a subtle box shadow: `boxShadow="md"`.

### 3.3 Chat Interface ("iMessage" Pattern)
- **User's Messages:** Right-aligned, Primary Blue background, white text.
- **Other Participant's Messages:** Left-aligned, gray background (`#E2E8F0`), black text.
- **AI Mediator's Messages:** Left-aligned, distinct avatar, unique subtle background (`#F3F4FF`).
